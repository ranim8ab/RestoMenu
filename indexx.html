At Ranim's Place

Appetizers

  • Caesar Salad (Mexico): Romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing,croutons and parmesan.
  • Spring Rolls (China/Southest Asia): Crispy rolls filled with vegetables or meat,served with dipping sauce.
  • French Onion Soup (France): Caramelized onions in broth,topped with toasted bread and cheese.
  • Hummus with Pita (Middle East): Creamy chickpea dip served with warm pita bread.

Non Vegitarians Main Courses

  • Sushi Platter (Japan): Assorted sushi rolls and nigiri with fresh seafood.
  • Fish and Ships (UK): Crispy fried fish served with golden fries.
  • Paella (Spain): Traditional Valencian rice dish cooked with seafood,chicken,saffron and vegetables.
  • Shawarma Plate (Middle East): Marinated meat roasted on a spit,served with rice,pita and sauces.
  • Classic Beef Burger (USA): Grilled beef patty in a bun with lettuce,tomato,cheese and sauce.
  • Spaghetti Bolognese (Italy): Pasta served with a rich slow-cooked meat and tomato sauce.
  • Pho (Vietnam): Fragrant beef or chicken noodle soup with herbs and spices.
  • Beef Straganoff (Russia): Tender beef strips in a creamy mushroom sauce,served over noodles or rice.

Vegitarians Main Courses

  • Pizza Margherita (Italy): Classic pizza with tomato sauce,mozzarella and basil.
  • Vegetable Stir-Fry (China): Mixed vegetables sautéed with soy sauce and seasonings.
  • Falafel Plate (Middle East): Crispy ground chickpea patties served with humus,salad and pita.
  • Ratatouille (France): Traditional Provençal stew made from tomatoes,zucchini,eggplant and peppers.
  • Vegetable Pad Thai (Thailand): Rice noodles stir-fried with tofu,vegetables,peanuts and tamarind sauce.
  • Aloo Gobi (India): Potato and coliflower cooked with turmeric,ginger and spices.

Desserts

  • Dolce de Leche (Argentina): Sweet caramel-like spread made by slowly heating milk and sugar.
  • Black Forest Cake (Germany): Chocolate cake layerd with cherries and whipped cream.
  • Mochi (Japan): Sweet rice cake with chewy texture,filled with icecream or sweet pastes.
  • Baklava (Middle East): Layers of filo pastry filled with nuts and sweetened with honey or syrup.
  • Eton Mess (UK): Crushed meringe mixed with strawberries and whipped cream.
  • Tarte aux Fruits (Italy): Pastry tart filled with custard and topped with fresh berries like strawberries,raspberries or blueberries.
